About

Max Miller is a writer from New England. Growing up in southern New Hampshire, Max's love of the outdoors and the horrors that may-or-may-not be lurking in the New England wilderness drove a passion for crafting stories that lurk under the surface of the innocuous. After graduating with a Bachelors of Science in Physics from the University of New Hampshire, Max moved to Boston MA where he now resides and works as a software engineer. Most days he can be seen skulking around his local coffee shop or bookstore, usually with a notebook and pen. On the weekends, he likes hiking with his friends in the White Mountains and visiting local Massachusetts nature trails. His work can be found in Wingless Dreamer, UNH's Aegis, and on his personal blog A Chair in the Void.
